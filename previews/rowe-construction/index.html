About Rowe

Central Illinois
Infrastructure

Rowe Construction Co. is a Bloomington, Illinois general contractor specializing in heavy civil and municipal infrastructure. We operate the equipment, manage the crews, and deliver the results — from initial grading through final paving.

Our work spans highways, bridges, commercial paving, site development, and concrete work across Central Illinois. We partner with municipalities, developers, and commercial clients who need a contractor that shows up and gets it done.
